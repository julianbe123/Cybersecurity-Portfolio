# IT Audit Case Study  
### Information Security & Compliance Assessment  
**Prepared by:** Julian Bechler  

---

## Executive Summary

This enterprise-level IT Audit was conducted on a mid-sized organisation operating in the financial/education sector.  
The assessment focused on Information Security, Governance, Asset Management, Network Security, and Operational Controls, aligned with ISO 21001 and ISO 27001 principles.

Key outcomes:
- 15 total findings  
- 7 high-risk issues  
- Systemic weaknesses in encryption, patching, audit cycles, asset recovery, RBAC, and documentation  

This case study follows Big Four methodology with ISO clause mapping, evidence-based observations, and structured remediation planning.

## Key Findings (Summary)

### High Risk
| # | Finding | Summary | ISO Clause |
|---|---------|---------|------------|
| 1 | Outdated Encryption | Weak crypto algorithms | 7.1.5 |
| 2 | Missing Patches | No recent updates on firewall/router/server | 8.1 |
| 3 | Unqualified Admin | Firewall managed by untrained personnel | 7.2 |
| 4 | Late Internal Audit | Audit delayed by 24 months | 9.2 |
| 5 | Unsecured Documents | Confidential data left exposed | 7.5.3 |
| 6 | Asset Not Returned | Laptop not recovered during offboarding | 8.5.3 |
| 7 | HR Access Breach | Unrestricted department access | 7.5.3.2 |

### Medium Risk
- ISMS documentation outdated  
- Risk assessment not updated  
- Security awareness not delivered  
- Backup frequency not enforced  
- Vendor controls weak  

### Low Risk
- Copyright misuse

## Remediation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Immediate (0–30 Days)
- Upgrade encryption algorithms  
- Reinstate quarterly patch cycles  
- Recover unreturned devices  
- Restart internal audit schedule  
- Enforce RBAC restrictions  
- Assign certified firewall administrator  

### Phase 2: Stabilisation (30–90 Days)
- Update and relabel ISMS documentation  
- Implement organisation-wide security awareness  
- Strengthen vendor maintenance oversight  

### Phase 3: Optimisation (90+ Days)
- Improve documentation governance  
- Establish data owners within departments
